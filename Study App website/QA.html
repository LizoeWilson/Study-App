Q&A

Who is [Mascot]?
[Mascot] is the face of the study app. He's a senior in high school who has a lot of homework, but finds working with others too be better than working alone.
His design is by Azaria. She was going for a simple easy-to-draw character to represent the app and give it personality.

What should I do if there is something wrong with the app?
If the app is not working properly you can try downloading the latest version of the app from the download page. The error may have been fixed in the latest update. If this error is in the latest version, please email us so that we can fix this error in the next update. You can find our email on the contact page.

When will the next update be released?
Updates are worked on when the developer has the time so there is no consistent deadline. If there is an update being worked on, it will be anounced along with an aproximation of its release.
