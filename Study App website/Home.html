This is the official website for Shep's Study App, the first app from Hub-Bub Studios.

Shep's Study App is an app specially designed to help students get the most out of their study sessions. It take's its inspiration from the mobile app Study Bunny. Shep's Study App has many features and is always being updated.

Right now, its features include:

  • a timer
  • a to-do list
  • in-app reminders (coming soon)
